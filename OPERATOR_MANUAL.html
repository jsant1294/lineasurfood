Getting Started

Your menu is live at a public URL. The admin panel is where you manage everything — no app installation required, works from any device.

Your URLs

Your customer-facing menu:

https://lineasur-menu.vercel.app

Your admin panel:

https://lineasur-menu.vercel.app/admin

Accessing the Admin Panel

  1. 1
    Open the admin URL in any browser
    Works on phone, tablet, or computer. Bookmark it for quick access.
  2. 2
    Enter your 6-digit PIN
    Your PIN was provided when the system was set up. Contact support if you've lost it.
  3. 3
    You're in — three tabs at the top
    Business · Menu · Orders. Start with Menu to review your items.
⚠️
Keep your PIN private Anyone with the PIN can edit your menu and business settings. Do not share it publicly.

Business Settings

Go to Admin → Business tab. Always hit Save Business at the bottom after making changes.

Identity

Business Name
Displayed as the main heading on your menu page and in the browser tab.
Tagline
Short phrase below your name. Keep it under 60 characters.
Logo
Square image. Tap the logo area, crop your photo, and it uploads automatically. Recommended: 400 × 400 px.
Default Language
ES (Spanish) or EN (English). Customers can always switch — this sets the opening language.

Contact & Location

WhatsApp Number
Include country code, no spaces or dashes. Example: 14045551234
Phone
Shown in the footer and as a tap-to-call link.
Address
Links to Google Maps automatically.
Service Areas
Comma-separated list of neighborhoods or cities you serve. Example: Norcross, Doraville, Duluth
Hours
Opens and Closes times control the "Open Now" badge on your storefront. Use 24-hour format.

Hero (storefront banner)

Photo
Upload a wide banner photo. Best size: 1200 × 675 px.
Poster
Use a flyer or promotional graphic as your banner.
Branded
No photo — uses your brand colors in an animated gradient pattern.
Hero Badge
A small italic label overlaid on the banner. Example: "Lo hacemos conveniente"

Theme Color

Choose from the color swatches in the Theme section. The color affects buttons, accents, and the OG share card. Hit Save after changing.

Menu & Items

Go to Admin → Menu tab. Changes are saved when you hit Save Menu at the bottom. Changes are visible to customers immediately after saving.

Adding a New Item

  1. 1
    Tap "Add Item" at the top
    A new card appears at the bottom of the list.
  2. 2
    Fill in the name in both languages
    Nombre (ES) and Name (EN) are both shown on the menu based on the customer's language.
  3. 3
    Set the category and price
    Category groups items on the menu (e.g. Entradas, Platos Fuertes, Bebidas). Price is in US dollars.
  4. 4
    Add a photo (recommended)
    Tap the image box → pick a photo → crop → wait for the upload spinner to finish before saving.
  5. 5
    Add descriptions (optional)
    Short descriptions in both languages appear in the item detail popup.
  6. 6
    Hit Save Menu
    You'll see "Saved ✓". Changes go live for all customers instantly.

Item Controls

ButtonWhat it does
Featured ★Highlights the item in the featured row at the top of the menu. Use for your best sellers.
Available / Sold OutToggles visibility. Sold Out items are greyed out and can't be ordered.
↑ ↓ arrowsReorders items within the list. Items appear on the menu in this order.
Copy iconDuplicates the item — useful for creating variations.
Trash iconPermanently removes the item. This cannot be undone.
💡
Tip Mark items "Sold Out" instead of deleting them when you run out for the day. It's faster to toggle back than to re-enter everything.

Modifiers

Modifiers let customers customize an item — choose a size, pick add-ons, or select extras that affect the price. They appear as tap-to-select chips in the item popup.

Setting Up a Modifier Group

  1. 1
    Find the item in the Menu tab
    Scroll to the item you want to add options to.
  2. 2
    Click "Modifiers ▼" to expand
    Below the description fields on each item card.
  3. 3
    Click "Add modifier group"
    Enter the group name in Spanish and English (e.g. "Tamaño" / "Size").
  4. 4
    Set Required and Max picks
    Required: customer must choose before adding to cart. Max picks: 1 = pick one only, 2 or 3 = pick up to that many.
  5. 5
    Add options with "Add option"
    Each option needs a name in both languages. Set a price delta (e.g. 1.50 for +$1.50). Leave at 0 for no extra charge.
  6. 6
    Save Menu when done
    Modifier selections appear in the WhatsApp order message and in your order log.

Examples

Extras / Add-onsNo3Aguacate +$1.50 · Queso +$1.00 · Jalapeños $0

💡
Tip Keep modifier groups to 3 or fewer per item. Too many choices slow down ordering. If you have many options, consider splitting into separate menu items.

Fulfillment & Orders

Go to Admin → Business → Fulfillment section to control how customers receive their orders.

Fulfillment Modes

Pickup
Customer comes to pick up. Shows your address at checkout.
Delivery
You deliver to customer. Asks for their address. Add a delivery fee if applicable.
Dine-In
Customer eats on-site. Asks for party size at checkout.

Order Minimums & Fees

Minimum Order
Set per fulfillment mode. Customer cannot submit if their cart is below this amount.
Delivery Fee
Added to the subtotal when delivery mode is selected. Set to 0 for free delivery.
Tax
Enable and enter your tax rate (%). Tax is calculated on the subtotal only, not on the delivery fee.

Time Slots

Optional feature. When enabled, customers pick a time window for their pickup or delivery. Set the interval (15 / 30 / 60 min) and the maximum orders per slot.

💡
Order flow reminder Customers submit via WhatsApp — your WhatsApp receives the order, you fulfill it, you respond to confirm. The app does not process payments; it is an ordering channel only.

Order Log

Go to Admin → Orders tab. Every checkout that a customer submits is logged here automatically.

  1. 1
    Today's orders load by default
    The summary shows total order count and revenue for the selected day.
  2. 2
    Use ← Prev / Next → to browse other dates
    Orders are stored by calendar day. Navigate backward to see historical orders.
  3. 3
    Tap "Show details" on any order
    Expands to show every item ordered, modifier selections, delivery address, time slot, notes, and payment method.
⚠️
Note The order log captures orders at the moment the customer presses the WhatsApp button. If the customer abandons the checkout screen without submitting to WhatsApp, the order is NOT logged.

The admin header has quick links to two print tools:

Share Card (OG Image)

When you share your menu URL on WhatsApp, iMessage, or social media, a preview card automatically appears showing your business name, tagline, and theme color. No setup needed — it updates automatically when you change your business name or theme.

💡
Print tip Use your browser's Print function (Ctrl+P / Cmd+P) on the QR or Flyer page. Choose "Save as PDF" for a file you can send or print at any shop.

Common Situations

SituationWhat to do
Item is temporarily unavailableToggle it to "Sold Out" in Menu tab. Tap again to make available. Hit Save Menu.
Wrong price on an itemEdit the price field → Save Menu. Updates instantly.
Need to add a temporary specialAdd Item, set as Featured ★, save. Remove or mark Sold Out when it's gone.
Orders not showing in logConfirm customer actually hit the WhatsApp button and submitted. If WhatsApp opened, the order was logged.
Image not updating on other devicesImages update in real time. If stale, have the customer do a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R / Cmd+Shift+R).
Lost admin PINContact your system provider to reset.

Add to Home Screen On iPhone: tap Share → "Add to Home Screen." On Android: tap the browser menu → "Add to Home Screen." The admin panel works like an app without needing to download anything.

Keeping Your Menu Fresh

The most effective menus are updated regularly. A few habits that help:

  • Mark sold-out items daily rather than leaving them visible
  • Update the hero badge or tagline for seasonal promotions
  • Rotate the Featured ★ items weekly to highlight different dishes
  • Add photos to every item — menus with photos get significantly more orders
